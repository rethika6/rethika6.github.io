About Computational Media

What is it?

Computational Media is the intersection of Computer Science and Design.

Courses

I take all of the Computer Science general education requirements while my upper level courses are focused on my threads of Artificial Intelligence and Interaction Design. One half of my coursework is with the college of Computing and the other is with the LMC School.

Interests

Future

The Computational Media major gives me the tools to work in many different fields. Some examples include working as a software engineer, mobile or web developer, or a UI/UX designer.

About Me

Hi! My name is Rethika Ambalam and I am a sophomore at the Georgia Institute of Technology. This is my first year at Tech as I transferred from the University of Tennessee in Knoxville.
The main reason I came to Tech was because I wanted to major in Compuational Media. This major allows me to study the intersection of technology and design. I have had an interest in design since I was litte. In my free time, I would learn how to make duct tape wallets and jewelry. I was entranced by crafting. Everything I did was always meticulous and measured. I followed youtube tutorials closely to the point where I now realize I was iterating through the steps and thinking about crafting through a computational lense. All of these experiences led me to where I am now.
Some of my hobbies include playing tennis, photography, and playing board games with friends and family. Fun fact, I took the photo you see on the main page. Furthermore, I love traveling, watching Formula 1 and watching TV. My current favorite TV show is Suits and my favorite movie is Now You See Me (I was a huge magic fanatic as a kid)!

Projects and Activities

CRRAFT Undergraduate Research Project

Research Assistant

At the University of Tennesse, Knoxville, I worked with Professor Freances Harper as an Undergraduate Research Assistant to help BIPOC children get access to computer science principles. To do this, I designed a gameboard consisting of a maze for a code-and-go robot mouse in Photoshop. The mat and the robot mouse come in kits that are given to parents and teachers to expose preschoolers to computational thinking.

DesignsbyRethika

Founder

DesignsbyRethika is a small business I started in highschool where I sold sticker designs on Redbubble. I also sold wooden letter photo collages and handmade greeting cards on Etsy. With this, I gained experience in marketing and developing an audience on social media platforms by teaching others how to use photoshop and marketing my products. I was able to reach over one hundred thousand monthly viewers on Pinterest with my Photoshop tutorials.

BetterReads

Designer

During a summer program, I designed a prototype of BetterReads with three other students using Figma. The goal was to create a more user friendly and design oriented version of the app GoodReads. As an avid reader, I love sharing what books I am currently reading with friends. When using the GoodReads app, I noticed that it was very outdated and hard to use for new users. Thus, we used Figma to design a potential upgrade to GoodReads.

Society of Women Engineers

Member

I became a member of the Society of Women engineers at the University of Tennesse and joined the chapter at Georgia Tech to continue working with them. With SWE, I have volunteered with members in organizations such as Girl Scouts. I am also participating in a mentorship program as a mentee to learn from upperclassmen studying Computer Science.
